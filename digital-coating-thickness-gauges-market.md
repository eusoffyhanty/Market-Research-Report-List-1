<p><p>Digital coating thickness gauges are essential tools for measuring the thickness of coatings on various substrates. Magnetic and eddy current technologies are typically used for ferrous and non-ferrous materials, respectively, allowing for precise measurements without damaging the surface. Ultrasonic technology employs sound waves to gauge thick coatings, ideal for applications like plastics and composites. X-ray technology offers non-destructive analysis through penetration, perfect for thick or layered coatings. Each method serves specific industry needs, ensuring quality control and compliance.</p></p>
