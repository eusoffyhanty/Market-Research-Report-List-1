<p><p>The fabric detergents market consists of two primary types: powder detergents and liquid detergents. Powder detergents are often cost-effective, offer a longer shelf life, and are effective in removing tough stains, particularly in hot water. Liquid detergents, on the other hand, are convenient, dissolve easily in cold water, and are favored for pre-treatment of stains. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with liquid options typically gaining popularity for their ease of use and effectiveness in modern washing machines.</p></p>
