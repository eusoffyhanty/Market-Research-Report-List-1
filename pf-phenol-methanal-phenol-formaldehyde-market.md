<p><p>The PF phenol-methanal market, commonly known as phenol-formaldehyde, is categorized into two main types: Novolac and Resol. Novolac resins are solid, thermosetting polymers typically requiring a hardening agent, primarily used in applications such as adhesive formulations and molding compounds. Resol resins, on the other hand, are liquid and can cure on their own through heat, making them suitable for applications in coatings and composites. Both types are widely utilized in various industries due to their strength and durability.</p></p>
