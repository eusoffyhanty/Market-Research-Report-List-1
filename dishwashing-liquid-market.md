<p><p>The dishwashing liquid market is primarily divided into two segments: hand dishwashing liquid and automatic dishwashing liquid. Hand dishwashing liquid is designed for manual cleaning of dishes, characterized by its ability to cut grease and provide effective cleaning with minimal water. In contrast, automatic dishwashing liquid is formulated for use in dishwashers, offering concentrated cleaning power to tackle tough stains and food residues, while also being low-foaming to prevent overflow in machines. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
