<p><p>The bottle sealing wax market encompasses two primary types: bottle sealing wax beads and bottle sealing wax bricks. Wax beads are small, easy-to-melt pellets favored for their convenience and precision, allowing for quick and controlled sealing applications. In contrast, wax bricks are larger solid forms that require more melting time and are ideal for bulk sealing needs, often preferred by businesses doing high-volume production. Both types are used to enhance bottle aesthetics and provide tamper-evidence.</p></p>
