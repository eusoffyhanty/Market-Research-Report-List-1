<p><p>The paper water bottle market can be categorized into two main types: fully paper made and partially paper made. Fully paper made bottles are constructed entirely from biodegradable paper materials, providing an eco-friendly alternative to plastic. These bottles aim to reduce plastic waste and are often designed to be compostable. Partially paper made bottles, on the other hand, incorporate paper in combination with other materials, typically plastic liners, to enhance durability and maintain liquid containment while still minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
