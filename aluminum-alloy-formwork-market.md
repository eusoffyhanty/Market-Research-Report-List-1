<p><p>The Aluminum Alloy Formwork market includes two main types: Screw Type and Pull Tab. Screw Type formwork uses threaded fasteners for secure assembly, offering high stability and load-bearing capacity, ideal for large-scale construction. Pull Tab formwork employs interlocking tabs for quick assembly and disassembly, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs, suitable for repetitive projects. Both types are lightweight, durable, and reusable, catering to modern construction needs for speed, precision, and sustainability.</p></p>
