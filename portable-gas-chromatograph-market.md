<p><strong>The Portable Gas Chromatograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-liquid Chromatograph</li><li>Gas-solid Chromatograph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable gas chromatograph market is primarily divided into two types: gas-liquid chromatographs and gas-solid chromatographs. Gas-liquid chromatography separates compounds based on their volatility and interaction with a liquid stationary phase, making it ideal for analyzing volatile organic compounds. In contrast, gas-solid chromatography utilizes a solid stationary phase to separate gases based on adsorption properties, effective for analyzing gases with varying affinities. Both types offer portability for on-site analysis in environmental monitoring, safety, and industrial applications.</p></p>
