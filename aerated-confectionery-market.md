<p><p>The aerated confectionery market is categorized into three main types based on the level of aeration: High Aerated Confectionery, Medium Aerated Confectionery, and Low Aerated Confectionery. High Aerated Confectionery features a significant amount of air incorporated, resulting in light, fluffy textures, such as meringues and marshmallows. Medium Aerated Confectionery has a balanced aeration, creating products like nougats and certain gummies. Low Aerated Confectionery contains minimal air, producing denser treats like chocolate and fudge, appealing to various consumer preferences.</p></p>
