<p><ul><li>Reduced Voltage AC Starters</li><li>Full Voltage AC Motor Starters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC motor starters market is primarily segmented into two types: Reduced Voltage AC Starters and Full Voltage AC Motor Starters. Reduced Voltage Starters, such as auto-transformer or star-delta starters, minimize starting current and torque, enhancing motor lifespan and efficiency. In contrast, Full Voltage Starters supply the complete voltage directly to the motor, providing immediate full power for applications requiring high torque from the outset. Each type caters to specific operational needs and application requirements in industrial settings.</p></p>
