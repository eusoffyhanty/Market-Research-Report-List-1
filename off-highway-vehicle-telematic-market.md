<p><p>The Off-Highway Vehicle Telematics market comprises two primary types: Cellular and Satellite. Cellular telematics utilizes mobile networks to provide real-time vehicle data, making it ideal for areas with good coverage, offering features like location tracking and diagnostics. Satellite telematics, on the other hand, operates independently of cellular networks, enabling communication in remote locations where cellular signals may be weak or nonexistent. This ensures continuous monitoring and data collection, making it essential for industries operating in isolated regions.</p></p>
